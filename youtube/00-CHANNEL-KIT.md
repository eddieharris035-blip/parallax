## Banner art spec (for Canva — ~2 min to make)

Size: **2560 × 1440 px** (YouTube standard; shows well on mobile + TV).

Simple design that matches the site:
- **Background:** Deep navy / near-black (hex `#070714` to match site)
- **Starfield:** Scattered white dots, varying sizes (Canva has a "stars" element)
- **Warm glow:** Soft orange radial blur in upper right (hex `#ffcc80`, low opacity)
- **Text left side, large:**
  - Line 1: **PARALLAX** (bold, serif — "Playfair Display" or "Lora" in Canva)
  - Line 2: *Curiosity, calculated* (italic, smaller, brand-orange `#ffcc80`)
- **Subtext below:**
  - "New videos every Saturday" (muted gray, smaller)
- **Tiny planet graphic** on right side (any dark-background planet silhouette Canva offers)

Canva search terms: "space banner," "dark minimalist," "astronomy."

## Monetization milestones (keep expectations real)

YouTube Partner Program requires **BOTH**:
- 1,000 subscribers
- 4,000 watch hours in the past 12 months

**OR** YouTube Shorts path:
- 1,000 subscribers
- 10,000,000 Short views in the past 90 days

For a channel posting once a week about curiosity calculators, realistic timeline:
- **Month 3-6:** First monetization if one video breaks out (100K+ views).
- **Month 12:** Monetized if consistent.
- **Year 2+:** Real revenue if you stick with it.

This is a slow-burn play. Don't expect fast payout. The real win is **traffic funneled to Parallax** where AdSense + future products can monetize.
