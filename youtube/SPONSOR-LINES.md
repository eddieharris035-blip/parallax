## YouTube upload settings (CRITICAL — do this every time)

When uploading any Parallax video:

1. **Details tab → "Paid promotion"** → ✅ **check the box** "This video contains paid promotion, like a paid product placement, sponsorship, or endorsement."

   *Required by YouTube/FTC even for self-sponsorship. Takes 1 second. Failure to check = violation risk.*

2. **Description:** paste the appropriate sponsor line from above

3. **Tags:** add `smart home` as a bonus tag — free crossover discovery
