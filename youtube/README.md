## If you want to quit

That's fine. 90% of people who start a YouTube channel stop within 3 months. No judgment.

**But do NOT quit based on video 1 or 2 performance.** The algorithm takes 4-8 weeks to even figure out what your channel is about. Early videos are data, not verdicts.

**The only acceptable quit reason before video 10:** "I genuinely hate doing this."

If it's just "it's not blowing up fast enough" — keep going. The first 10 videos are the cost of entry.
