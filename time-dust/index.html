How this is calculated

We take your age in days, multiply each daily habit in hours, and convert the total back to years. It assumes the pattern stays roughly constant — nobody sleeps exactly 8 hours every night since birth, so these are rough strokes, not clinical estimates.

The goal isn't precision. It's perspective.
