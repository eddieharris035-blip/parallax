Privacy Policy

Last updated: .

Plain-English summary

We don't collect personal information. Everything you enter into the calculators (birth dates, hours, etc.) is processed entirely in your browser and stored only on your device. We don't run analytics. The one exception: Google AdSense, which shows the ads on this site, may place cookies and collect information as described below.

Information we collect

From you directly: none. We don't ask for an email, name, or account. There are no sign-ups.

Stored on your device: the calculators use your browser's localStorage to remember the birth date you entered, so you don't have to retype it when moving between tools. This stays on your device. Clearing your browser's site data removes it.

From the server: standard web-server logs (IP address, user agent, requested URL, timestamp) may be kept briefly by our hosting provider. These are not linked to any identity by us.

Google AdSense and advertising cookies

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites on the web.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this and other sites.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.
  • For more information on how Google uses data: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Cookies

This site does not set its own tracking cookies. Cookies set by Google AdSense are covered by Google's own privacy policy, linked above. If you are in a region where cookie consent is required (EU/UK/CA), a consent notice will be shown on first visit.

Children's privacy

Parallax is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided information here, please contact us.

Your rights

Because we don't store personal information on our side, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete. You can clear your device's data at any time via your browser settings. Requests related to data processed by Google AdSense should be directed to Google.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top. Material changes will be noted on the homepage for at least 30 days.
