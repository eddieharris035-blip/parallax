Why Parallax?

Parallax is the tiny shift in an object's apparent position when you look at it from different angles. These tools do the same thing with your life — small shifts of perspective that change what you notice.

Every calculator runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent anywhere. No accounts, no tracking, no sign-up.
